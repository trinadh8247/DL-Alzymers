Dataset details

Source: Kaggle

Provenance

Alzheimer's MRI multiclass dataset (equalized and augmented) by Aryan Singhal, hosted on Kaggle. Images are pre-processed T1-weighted axial MRI slices organized by clinical stage.

Label schema

Four classes representing progressive cognitive impairment: Non Demented, Very Mild Demented, Mild Demented, and Moderate Demented. The notebook documents any relabeling or class balancing applied before training.

Preprocessing

Uniform resize to 224×224, RGB conversion, and ImageNet mean/std normalization to match VGG16 expectations. Augmentations (flips/rotations) equalize class counts; see the notebook for exact transforms.

Model training summary

VGG16 backbone with ImageNet weights, later conv blocks fine-tuned, custom classifier head with batch norm and dropout. Training uses cross-entropy on the balanced set.

Responsible use

For research and educational purposes. MRI-based screening should be paired with clinical assessment, additional biomarkers, and oversight from medical professionals.

Project summary

End-to-end pipeline

Goal

Provide a transparent, reproducible demo of MRI-based Alzheimer's stage classification, from dataset to deployable Flask app.

Stack

PyTorch + torchvision VGG16, PIL/NumPy preprocessing, Flask backend, vanilla JS frontend.

Outputs

Stage prediction with confidence, top-3 breakdown, and full class probability bars. Downloadable model and notebook for further study.

Next steps

Extend with external validation, calibration, and clinician-in-the-loop review; optionally swap backbone to lighter MobileNet for faster inference.
